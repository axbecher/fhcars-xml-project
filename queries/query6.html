Doar masinile Hypercars realizate in anul 2019, ordonate in functie de pretul acestora

xQuery

let $objects := (doc("FH_Cars.xml")/Object/GameObject)
for $x in $objects
where $x/Model_type = 'HYPERCARS' and $x/Name_and_model[contains(.,'2019')]
order by $x/In_Game_Price descending
return ...
