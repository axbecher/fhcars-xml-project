Masini care au rating peste 7 la viteza, control, acceleratie, lansare, franare, ordonate in functie de acceleratie, descrescator.

xQuery

let $objects := (doc("FH_Cars.xml")/Object/GameObject)
for $x in $objects
where $x/speed >= '7' and $x/handling >= '7' and $x/acceleration >= '7' and $x/launch >= '7' and $x/braking >= '7'
order by $x/acceleration descending
return ...
